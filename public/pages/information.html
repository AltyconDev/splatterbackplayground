Events are announced the week of the event and can be cancelled due to any storm like conditions. If rain continues for an extended amount of time on the day of the event, the event will be cancelled.

Track Hours

Saturday: Open for announced events only.

Weekdays & Sunday: CLOSED

Pricing

Riders: $35.00

Spectators: $5.00

All spectators and riders are required to sign wavers.

All minors MUST be accompanied by a parent/legal guardian.

WEAPONS, DRUGS, and ALCOHOL are prohibited!
