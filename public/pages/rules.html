Splatterback Playground's number one priority is always the safety of our riders and spectators. Splatterback's mission is to have a family oriented, safe and fun riding environment for the motorcross and supercross community. All safety rules and requirements are intended to keep Splatterback Playground, riders, spectators and families safe. Please read all of the rules and requirements!!!!

GEAR REQUIREMENTS

  • Helmet

  • Jersey

  • Pants

  • Boots

  • Goggles

    (optional)

  • Gloves

    (optional)

  • Chest
    Protector

    (optional)

  1. All spectators & riders are required to sign waivers.
  2. Always be aware of your surroundings.
  3. Maintain a safe speed in the parking lot & all spectator areas.
  4. Be sure to look both ways before entering the track.
  5. Be careful while passing other riders.
  6. Do not ride ouside of the track limits, stay on designated track when riding. NO RIDING IN FIELDS!!
  7. Ride within your limits.
  8. Respect & take care of the property & people. NO LITTERING!!
  9. Properly dispose of trash in the provided trash cans.
  10. Unsportsman-like conduct will not be tolerated!
  11. This is a practice track. Please be courteous to our inexperienced riders & children.
  12. All minors MUST be accompanied by a parent/legal guardian.
  13. NO TRESPASSING AFTER TRACK HOURS!!!
  14. WEAPONS, DRUGS & ALCOHOL ARE PROHIBITED!
